How to help?

There are many ways that we could be of help to support causes. We could volunteer at organizations and we could donate money or good to charities to support those in need. We could also help in advocating the cause that we are concerned.

Don`t underestimate the time, money and effort you pay. Every bit helps to create a better quality of life for the elderly people!

Join us!

 

 

 

Volunteering

We are currently seeking volunteers to assist us with website maintenance and provide support to elderly members of the community. As many younger individuals are occupied with work and other responsibilities, elderly individuals often find themselves without adequate resources to address their inquiries and concerns. Our volunteers would play a crucial role in answering basic questions about navigating the website, finding the information they need, and providing them with specific hotline to call or connect them with supporting services.

 

Volunteering & Donating

There are many organizations that help to support and feed the elderly people, and welcome your financial and item donations. Apart from donations, you may volunteer with your time and effort.




Advocate

Improving the quality of life for the elderly population in Hong Kong is a crucial issue that requires the participation and support of the community. There are various ways for individuals to get involved and make a difference, including participating in public polls, providing feedback through forums, taking advantage of broadcasting opportunities, and reflecting on issues to district counselors and the government.


Public polls are effective way to gather the opinions of the community and provide valuable insights to policy-makers. By participating in these polls, individuals can voice their concerns and advocates for issues that affect the elderly population in Hong Kong.


Feeding forums provide a platform for individuals to share their thoughts and suggestion on how to improve the quality of life. People engage in constructive dialogue and work together to find solutions to the challenges faced by the elderly people.


Broadcasting opportunities can be used to raise awareness and educate the public on issues affecting the elderly population. By sharing their experiences and insights, individuals can help to promote understanding and empathy towards the elderly people.

Reflecting on issues to district counselors and the government is an important way to advocate for change. By reaching out to these officials, individuals can express their concerns and call for action to improve the lives of the elderly people.
