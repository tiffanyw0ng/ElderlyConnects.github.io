Food Angel

Every week, Food Angel rescues 45 tones of edible surplus food, which results in the production of over 20,000 meals. For food assistance, contact:

Elderly Food Assistance in Sham Shui Po: Whatsapp 9404 1916
Family Food Assistance in Sham Shui Po: Whatsapp 5481 9932
Others Food Assistance Enquiries: Tel 2801 5333

Feeding HK

Each day over 3,400 tons of food waste is disposed of in landfills. Meanwhile, over 1 million people (14% of HK population) live in poverty struggling to afford nutritious meals. Feeding HK is supporting 150+ registered frontline local charities, which include senior homes, community centers, central kitchens and other food banks.

Brother Ming

Chan Cheuk-ming, also known as Ming-gor (brother Ming), is the owner of Pei Ho Barbecue Restaurant. The restaurant is best known to local customers for its tasty yet affordable meals, which it has been serving since 1983. At present, over 1,000 lunchboxes are distributed free on Mondays, Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays.
