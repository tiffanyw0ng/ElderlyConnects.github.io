Employment


As the population continues to age and life expectancy increases, there is a growing recognition of the valuable contribution that elderly individuals can make in the workforce. In line with this, the HKSAR government has taken steps to encourage organizations to hire elderly people.

 

 

 

Eligibility


Our employment services are offered to job seekers who are legally employable in HKSAR, including those holding Hong Kong Identity Card OR valid travel document with an endorsement by the HK Immigration Department for employment in Hong Kong within a specific period which has not yet expired.


HKSAR government has launched a website for elderly people and middle-aged people to browse and search suitable vacancies or information on job fairs anytime and anywhere.
