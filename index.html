About Us

We are a team of dedicated volunteers who are determined to make a difference to help the elderly population in Hong Kong. The income discrepancy in Hong Kong is huge. It is one of the worst among developed nations. One in five people sit below poverty line (before taking government aid into account). Some poor elderly individuals are cage residents and majority of the homeless people are elderly.

Although government and some organizations are providing services and facilities to assist elderly people to enjoy a quality life, elderly people usually find difficult in locating relevant information online. To assist elderly people to easily locate the services they need, we build a comprehensive and easily accessible website to provide ample information in one place. We also establish a whatsapp line to devote in helping elderly people for any enquiry they have when browsing the website.


We will regular update our website, please come back from time to time.
